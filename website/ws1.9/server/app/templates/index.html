About Scurdex

Scurdex is a digital asset wallet for trading various financial instruments on decentralized finance(DeFi) systems. The financial instruments are real-world Mirrored Assets(mAsset) of equities, mortgage, forex, commodities, fixed income instruments and over three thousand cryptocurries on Mirror Protocol. Scurdex also offer various decentralized inclusive financial services e.g. p2p payment, credit, insurance, mortgage, pension and forex auction. Scurdex does not offer savings account service and does not store your assets on any remote server. You are in control of your assets directly on-chain through your passphrase

As a decentralized app for asset management, it packs features designed to onboard investment-oriented individuals into a blockchain ecosystem without headaches. The idea is to give users one-stop-shop and tool for personal wealth creation. Pre-qualized and accreditated investors, by our backoffice fund managers or quants, are provided bespoke quantitative investment management service to deliver premium investment return with statistical arbitrage, mathematical models, algorithm trading and various risk management techniques.

Q4/2021 - Crypto Wallet on DEX & DeFi

Scurdex Wallet on three DEXs with DeFi service support was launched in September, 2021. It is a robust and feature rich non-custodial, multi-wallet and decentralized app for Android OS. It is DeFi (COMP, AAVE, UNISWAP, etc) enabled with WalletConnect and packaged with various crypto markets analytics suitable for investment oriented traders.

It provides users global access to over 2000+ coins and tokens of various types including Blockchains (Bitcoins, Zcash, Litecoin, DASH, Bitcoin Cash, Binance & Ethereum), Wallets, DeFi, etc. Scurdex has a built-in privacy feature enabled with TOR and with no back-end server except market data provider CoinGecko.io. Scurdex support ENS domains(e.g .wallet, .crypto) and provide blockchain education through Fante Academy and various FAQs.
