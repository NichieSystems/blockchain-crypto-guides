About Scurdex

Scurdex is a digital wallet for commission-free trading of over 2000 cryptocurrencies and investment in security tokens of non-crypto, high-growth potential and innovative startups solving healthcare, housing, food security and electricity supply challenges in developing countries. Scurdex is an acronym that represents Security and Currency Decentralized Exchange. It also support various microfinancial services including payment, credit, insurance, mortgage, pension and forex auction.

As a decentralized asset management tool for various financial instruments that are immune to inflation, confiscation, or theft, it packs features designed to onboard investment-oriented individuals into a blockchain ecosystem without headaches. The idea is to give you one-stop-shop and tool for your personal wealth creation. You don't need a finance degree from Havard or years of work experience with investment banks, hedge funds or stock brokering firms to be able to create an excellent portfolio of financial instruments with more than average return on investment.

Roadmap of Scurdex Features Rollout

With Scurdex Roadmap, in 2022, finance will be fully decentralized and made available for all, no matter the class or creed.

Q4/2021 - Cryptos DEX Launched

Scurdex Crypto DEX Trading was launched in September, 2021. It is a robust and feature rich non-custodial, multi-wallet and decentralized exchange for Android OS. It is DeFi (COMP, AAVE, UNISWAP, etc) enabled with Wallet Connect and packaged with various crypto markets analytics suitable for investment oriented traders.

It provides users global access to over 2000+ coins and tokens of various types including Blockchains (Bitcoins, Zcash, Litecoin, DASH, Bitcoin Cash, Binance & Ethereum), Wallets, DeFi, etc. Scurdex has a built-in privacy feature enabled with TOR and with no back-end server except market data provider CoinGecko.io. Scurdex support ENS domains(e.g .wallet, .crypto) and provide blockchain education through Fante Academy and various FAQs.
